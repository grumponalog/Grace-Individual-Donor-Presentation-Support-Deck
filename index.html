Three things worth understanding
The stakes.
01
Demand grew 25% last year. Another 25% is projected ahead.

This is not a hypothetical future problem. It is the situation right now.

02
Nearly 1 million pounds of food enter the waste stream in Richland County each year.

The problem is not supply. It is infrastructure. Grace is positioned to be the bridge.

03
Food insecurity in childhood is not something children recover from.

The brain requires 20% of daily energy to develop normally. The window does not reopen.

Why it moves Mansfield
Where a community invests
defines what it's becoming.

Grace is infrastructure. When families have food, children learn. Adults pursue stability. Every other investment in Mansfield's people becomes more effective.

This investment is a signal. Where a community invests defines what kind of city it is becoming. This says Mansfield is being rebuilt with its struggling residents, not around them.

The endowment component is what makes this investment last. $1M for operations, staffing, and long-term sustainability, not dependent on the goodwill of any single year.
